# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: June 13, 2026**

## Welcome to Timein

Timein ("the App") is designed to help users track their time, work sessions, breaks, and activities. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your information.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

Timein may store information you enter into the app, including:

- Activity names
- Categories
- Time tracking records
- Notes related to tracked sessions

### Automatically Collected Information

The App may collect limited technical information such as:

- Device type
- Operating system version
- App version
- Crash reports and diagnostics

This information helps improve app performance and stability.

## How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Provide time tracking functionality
- Generate statistics and productivity insights
- Improve app performance
- Fix bugs and technical issues

## Data Storage

Your time tracking data is stored locally on your device unless you choose to use future cloud synchronization features.

## Third-Party Services

Timein may use Apple services and analytics tools that collect information according to their own privacy policies.

Examples may include:

- Apple App Store
- Apple iCloud (if enabled in future versions)
- Apple Analytics

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

We may share information only when:

- Required by law
- Necessary to protect rights or safety
- Required for app functionality through trusted service providers

## Children's Privacy

Timein is not directed toward children under 13 years of age.

## Data Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.
